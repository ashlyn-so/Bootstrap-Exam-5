Dell Laptop

Source: Shielder95, Dell Inspiron 1525 - Wikipedia, 25 June 2018, en.wikipedia.org (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dell_Inspiron_1525)

Dell laptops have a high graphic video card built into them; they have an 128-bit hardware accelerated video support. The first Dell laptop was the DELL 316LT, which had 84-keys and weighed 15 pounds. The model 316LT was $3,499 in 1990(about $6,729 today). The screen size was 9.5-inch LCD and was 12.6 x 14.5 x 3 inches. In a 1990 roundup of laptop machines, InfoWorld remarked of the 316LT: "Fastest speed; lowest price," but complained about short battery life. The Dell laptops are developed and marketed by the computer manufacturer Dell.

The Dell 316LT had a 16 MHz Intel processor with 8 MB. It has one serial, one parallel port, one 8-bit half card PC compatible card slot, and external keyboard. It had a graphics resolution of 640 x 480 and was powered by battery and DC power supply.
