Microsoft Surface

Source: Joshua Topolsky, Microsoft Surface review - The Verge, 23 October 2018, theverge.com (https://www.theverge.com/2012/10/23/3540550/microsoft-surface-review)

Microsoft surface currently has seven generations. The First generation of Microsoft surface laptops were announced 2 May 2007 at the MicrosoftEDU event by Microsoft. It is a traditional laptop with a display fixed to a keyboard base. It began shipping on 17 June 2017. The Display was 13,5 QHD (2256 x 1504), and the cost was $799 to $2,699 USD. It had an aspect ratio of 3:2 with 3.4 million pixels.

The surface laptop had up to 1TB SSD, memory with up to 16 GB. The design weighed 1252 g and had dimensions of 308.1 x 223.2 x 14.4 mm. It supports up to 14.5 hours of battery life with a 45.2 Wh Lithium-ion unremovable battery.

Only Microsoft can repair or upgrade the laptop surface as it can't be opened without destroying it.
